**Priority 1 - Revenue & Conversion Impact**

1. **First-Order Welcome Discount (10-15%)**
   - 70%+ of competitors offer this
   - Newsletter signup trigger

   - Implementation: 1-2 days

2. **Live Chat Support**
   - 50%+ of competitors have it
   - Tidio, LiveChat, or Crisp integration
   - Can start with chatbot + human fallback
   - Implementation: 1 week

3. **Trustpilot/Review Platform Integration**
   - 55%+ of competitors use Trustpilot
   - Verified purchase reviews build trust
   - Critical for new customer acquisition
   - Implementation: 1-2 weeks

4. **Same-Day / Express Shipping Options**
   - 60%+ offer expedited shipping
   - "Order before 2PM EST for same-day dispatch"
   - Partner with FedEx 2-Day Air or Purolator Express
   - Implementation: 2-3 weeks (logistics setup)

5. **Affiliate/Referral Program Enhancement**
   - 45%+ have formal affiliate programs
   - BioCycle has referral points but no affiliate commission structure
   - 10-20% commission standard in industry
   - Implementation: 2-4 weeks

6. **Cryptocurrency Payment Options**
   - 40%+ of competitors accept crypto
   - Bitcoin + Ethereum minimum
   - NOWPayments or Blocknomics integration
   - Critical for the peptide industry (high-risk merchant category)
   - Implementation: 1-2 weeks
